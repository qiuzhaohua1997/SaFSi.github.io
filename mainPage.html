About Us

The SaFSi (Satellite Frequency Signal Integrity) Academic Research Group is dedicated to pioneering research in satellite communication technologies. Our mission is to address critical challenges related to satellite signal management, security, and performance in an increasingly complex and interconnected world.

Research Areas

  • Satellite Frequency Analysis

    Comprehensive studies on frequency management and optimization to ensure efficient and interference-free satellite communication.

  • Coexistence in Multiple Scenarios

    Developing strategies for seamless operation of satellite systems in shared frequency environments, minimizing conflicts and maximizing efficiency.

  • Satellite Signal Identification

    Advanced algorithms for identifying and distinguishing satellite signals to maintain security and signal integrity.

  • Signal Protocol Inversion

    Reverse engineering of signal protocols to analyze and enhance satellite communication systems while ensuring robust security measures.

Our Team

We are a group of dedicated researchers and engineers with expertise in satellite communications, signal processing, and security protocols. Our multidisciplinary approach enables us to tackle complex problems and drive innovation in the field.
